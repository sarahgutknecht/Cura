Rejuvenating Blend

This Blend of Essential Oils is for patients experiencing fatigue. Chemotherapy and radiation treatments sap your energy, making it hard to focus and go about daily life. Essential oils can give you the mental boost you need to get moving. This blend is sure to make your body and mind more awake and alert with its use of Peppermint, Lemon, and Rosemary oils!
